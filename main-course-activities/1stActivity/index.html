EMMAN NIMEDEZ

YouTuber | Director | Actor | Filmmaker

About

Actor, YouTuber, and filmmaker who was most well known for his self-titled YouTube channel where he released a number of short films, parodies, and covers. His films and vlogs earned him more than 2 million YouTube subscribers.

Before Fame

He studied broadcast communication at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.

Trivia

A television actor, he played Emman Abenida in the teen comedy series Luv U.

Family Life

He was raised with a sister named Loida and a brother named Rhafael.

Associated With

Roger Raker and Cong TV appeared in his music video parody "Kriminal - O.C Dawgs," posted in April 2016. The video earned more than 1 million views.
